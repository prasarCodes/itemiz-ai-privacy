Privacy Policy for Itemiz AI

Last updated: January 2025

Itemiz AI is a WhatsApp-based household inventory assistant that helps users manage grocery items, understand consumption patterns, and maintain shared group inventories.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Messages you send to the bot (text or images)
  • Inventory data you add, consume, or query
  • Group membership information

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Processing inventory-related commands
  • Providing insights, suggestions, and daily tips
  • Improving item recognition and app performance
  • Maintaining shared group inventories

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We only share information when required to provide the service (e.g., WhatsApp API message delivery).

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data.

5. Data Retention

Your inventory information is stored until you request deletion. You may request deletion at any time by messaging “delete my data” to the bot.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us via the bot: Send: “help”.
