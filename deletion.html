Data Deletion Instructions for Itemiz AI

Last updated: January 2025

You may request deletion of all your data stored by Itemiz AI at any time.

How to Delete Your Data

To delete your data, simply send the following message to the Itemiz AI WhatsApp bot:

delete my data

We will remove:

  • Your phone number
  • Your inventory data
  • Your group membership records
  • Your activity logs

Manual Deletion Request

If the automated command fails, contact us via WhatsApp by sending:

help

Your request will be processed within 48 hours.
